Dream

If I stopped making games, one of my dreams is to work in VR and AI.

Of course, a huge part of that is because of Sword Art Online.

When I was small, I once watched a video of a woman, Jan Scheuerman, using her brain to move an artificial arm.

I've always thought that could be implemented in the sector of games.

Playing games solely through your brain is one of the first steps in dive tech, that's what I think at least.

I would first start with a simple mario-like platformer game that would be played entirely hands-free, solely through thinking.

After optimizations and reducing the burden the brain needs to play, I would start looking into dreams.

If I'm not wrong, what is needed in dive technology would be found in researching the brain when one dreams.
